Most Searched Teams

Harvard University

T Harvard women’s rugby began as a club sport in the 1980s, fueled by the passion and dedication of students who loved the game despite limited resources. Over time, the team grew in both skill and recognition, laying the foundation for what would become one of the most successful women’s rugby programs in the United States.

Hamilton High School

Hamilton High School is a consistent powerhouse in high school rugby, with numerous state and national titles to its name. The program is renowned for developing young athletes who go on to achieve success at collegiate and professional levels.

Colorado Gray Wolves

The Colorado Gray Wolves have become a dominant force in the Women's Premier League, showcasing exceptional skill and teamwork. With four championship titles, they are a symbol of excellence in professional women's rugby.
