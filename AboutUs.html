About Us

We are a passionate team dedicated to promoting and empowering women in rugby. Our mission is to create a supportive community that celebrates the achievements and growth of women in this exciting sport.

Through education, events, and collaboration, we strive to inspire women to pursue their passion for rugby and break barriers in the sports world.

Join us in celebrating the strength, resilience, and camaraderie that make rugby a truly unique and empowering experience.

Mission Statement

To empower women through the game of rugby by fostering inclusion, resilience, and teamwork. We are committed to inspiring future generations and promoting equality in sports.

Our mission is to break stereotypes, build confidence, and cultivate a global community that supports women in achieving their potential both on and off the field.
